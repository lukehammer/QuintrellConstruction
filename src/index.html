About Us

Quintrell Construction is a family-owned company based in Woodland, Washington that specializes in new home construction and remodeling services. With 40 years of experience in the industry, the company has established itself as a trusted provider of high-quality construction services in the region.

Quintrell Construction takes great pride in its work and strives to exceed its clients' expectations on every project. The company offers a wide range of services, including new home construction, remodeling, kitchen and bath renovations, room additions, and custom home building. Whether you are looking to build your dream home from scratch or remodel your existing home to better suit your needs, Quintrell Construction has the expertise and experience to make it happen.

In addition to its commitment to quality craftsmanship, Quintrell Construction also places a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction. The company works closely with clients throughout the construction process to ensure that their vision for their home is realized to the fullest. The team at Quintrell Construction is dedicated to delivering exceptional results, on time and within budget, while providing a positive and stress-free experience for its clients.
